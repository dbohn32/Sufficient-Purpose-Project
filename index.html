Sufficient Purpose Project

Our Objective

Hello and thank you for visiting Sufficient Purpose. This is a community service program located in South Bend that is available through the donation process. Our purpose is to give to women and children in need who cant afford to purchase new clothing. We offer care packages to assist those in need. The contents of the care packages will range from what has been donated and is available at time of pickup. The contents of the package will range from food, sanitary items, and clothes. We offer two pick up locations located conveniently in the South Bend. If you are interested in a package please click on the desired location below for directions.
